FAQ¶

  1. Where is the point-source coupling phase and amplitude loss due to the mismatch between the beam in radiation and reception, that Shahab calculates at the lens surface?

  1. It is included in the main beam efficiency. These losses reduce the coupling to a point source, but the power (in transmission) couples to the sky.
